Tips for Best Results

  • Use high-contrast images: Clear separation between light and dark areas works best.
  • Convert to black and white: The generator uses a grayscale version of your image, so converting it to black and white first can give you more control over the final look.
  • Crop your image: Focus on the main subject of your image for a more detailed result.
  • Simplify the background: A simple or single-color background will help the main subject stand out.
